Product Description

The Pickle Ball Kit will give plenty of hours of fun. Pickle Ball is like a mixture of tennis rules/gameplay, ping-pong paddles, and wiffle ball balls. The love for this fairly new sport grows daily. Now you can add to it.

The net used in this kit is made from a nylon mixture. This means it is meant to last for years without wear and tear. The balls are made of premium-quality hard plastic and the paddles are made with a mixture of graphite, fiberglass, carbon fiber, and wood. These materials help generate a certain amount of power with control when swinging them.
