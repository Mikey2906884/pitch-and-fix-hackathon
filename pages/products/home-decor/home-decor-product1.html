Product Description

Our Touch to Light Lamp is designed for convenience and style. With a simple touch, you can adjust the brightness to your liking, making it perfect for reading, writing, or creating a cozy atmosphere in your home.

The touch-sensitive base is made from high-quality materials. It includes a metal plate with thin insulating coating that is connected to an electronic circuit that detects changes in capacitance when the metal plate is touched.
